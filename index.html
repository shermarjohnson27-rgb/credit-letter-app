Under the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), 15 U.S.C. § 1679, you have certain rights.
Your Rights Under Federal Law:

You have a right to dispute inaccurate information in your credit report without the help of a credit repair organization.

You may review your credit report without charge on a yearly basis. You are entitled to a free copy from AnnualCreditReport.com.

You have the right to sue a credit repair organization that violates the Credit Repair Organizations Act. This law prohibits deceptive practices by credit repair organizations.
